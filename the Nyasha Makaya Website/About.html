Education

Nyasha Attained his primary school education at a number of schools, namely:

  1. Sayi Primary School
  2. Gokwe St Agness Primary School
  3. Kubenengurira Primary School
  4. Mapfungautsi Primary

He went on to complete his Ordinary Level High School Education at Cheziya Gokwe High School, graduating with an honors in 2019.

In 2020, Nyasha joined the USAP Community School under the United Students Achievers program to complete his Advanced Level Classes.
During His time at USAPCS, he partaked in various activities and organizations eventually becoming a RISE Global Winner, and a Yale Young African Scholar.

Upon graduating high school, Nyasha was accepted into the prestigious California Institute of Technology for his undergraduate studies.
He is currently in his sophomore year of college, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Information and Data Science.
